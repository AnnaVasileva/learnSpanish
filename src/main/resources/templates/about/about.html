About Us

LearnSpanish

LearnSpanish is an educational website for learning Spanish language in its beginner level. This means that the platform could help you to be able to interact in a simple way, ask and answer questions about yourself, the place you live, the people you know and your possessions.
Furthermore, you will be able to construct simple statements about your immediate needs or daily issues. You will be able to introduce yourself and others, ask for and give basic personal information on your residence, your belongings and persons you know.

Creator

The creator of LearnSpanish is Anna Vasileva, a student in "St. Kliment Ohridski" University of Sofia, in the Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics (FMI).
This website was developed in order to finish her education for a master's degree. Anna Vasileva's main goal is to introduce a funny way to learn one of the most used languages in the world.
